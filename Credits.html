This little app lets you rename images in a folder so that they are named to sort alphabetically in the order you have them listed.


In other words, the first image in your list will be named 00001_image.jpg, the second will be named 00002_anotherImage.jpg, and so on.


You can arrange the images by dragging and dropping them in the list. If you just want to try your luck, there's a handy randomize button.


If there's any demand for it, I'd be happy to open-source this app. Just let me know.
